1. 为什么我在充值的时候，提示我“该号码不可充流量”？
答：根据相关规定，对于部分如非3G号码/欠费/非实名制/运营商黑名单用户，暂时不能使用流量充值服务。对于实名制等业务的办理方法，建议联系归属地运营商。
2. 流量充错号码怎么办？
答：非常抱歉，充错号码后运营商（移动、联通、电信）是不会办理退款的。您可以选择如下几种方式尝试弥补损失：1、联系实际充值的号码机主，与对方协商是否愿意为此补偿您的流量；2、联系运营商客服（移动10086，联通10010，电信10000），咨询是否能够退还已经充值成功的流量。给您带来的不便，尽请谅解！并希望您下次操作的时候注意核对号码是否正确，谢谢您的支持！
3. 我充值后怎么查看是否到账？
答：充值后，请以运营商信息提示为准，您也可以通过拨打运营商电话确认到账情况。
4. 为什么我充值后，使用流量还被运营商扣了额外的费用？
答：充值完成后，第三方中间服务商可能因为网络原因没有及时将充值电子订单发送至运营商，运营商对您的充值情况不知情。运营商会在收到第三方服务方订单后为您充值，并在成功后为您发送到账短信。请确保您在收到到账短信后再使用流量，以防出现流量包外的费用。
5. 充值的流量可以漫游吗？
答：充值时请关注页面提示，如果显示“全国可用”表示可以全国漫游。
6. 充值的流量有效期是多长？
答：大部分省份运营商充值流量当月有效，月底失效，部分面额30天内有效及三个月有效，灵活账期用户月结日失效。请以短信通知及在运营商处查询的信息为准。
7. 充值流量后能取消吗？
答：不可以。充值流量接近实时交易，支付完成后，交易系统会在数秒中向运营商发起充值请求并且充值到账。充值中的订单也会锁定，无法进行资金回退交易。您需要在充值前确定需要充值流量。
8. 充值失败后，为什么我没收到退款？
答：通常情况下，充值失败时我们会立即为您办理退款。如24小时内退款未到账，您可以拨打客服电话：400-819-9910。
9. 为什么我的号码一直充值失败？
答：由于联通存在每个面额每个月充值5次的限额，充值超过5次（不限微信平台）将会出现失败退款。建议您充值其他面额的流量。另外，号码欠费、套餐互斥、非实名认证、运营商黑名单等原因也会导致充值失败退款。
10. 如何查询订单或交易记录？
答：流量充值的页面的右上角，可以查看所有订单和交易记录。
11. 为什么我在月初月末充值经常充值失败？
答：由于月初月末为出账期，运营商结算，系统繁忙，流量充值可能会失败。请避高峰期进行购买。
1. Why does the message “Unable to top up data for this number” appear when I try to top up my data?
ANS: According to relevant regulations, some users, such as those using non-3G numbers/those who are behind on their payments/non-real-name system users/blacklisted users, are not permitted to use the data top-up service for now. For matters pertaining to the real-name system and other businesses, please contact the operator in your region.
2. What should I do if I topped up data to the wrong phone number?
ANS: We are very sorry, but no refund will be made by the operators (China Mobile, China Unicom, China Telecom) if data was topped up to the wrong number. You can try to minimize your loss through one of the following ways: 1. Contact the owner of the number that actually received the top-up, and request for compensation for the topped up data; 2. Contact the service center of your operator (China Mobile - 10086, China Unicom - 10010, China Telecom - 10000) and ask if you can get a refund on the topped up data. We apologize for any inconvenience that this may have caused you. Please be sure to double check the number for your next top-up. Thank you for your understanding!
3. How do check if my top-up has been credited?
ANS: After topping up, your operator will send you a message to confirm. You can also call your operators to verify if the top-up has been credited.
4. After I topped up my data, why am I still being charged for data usage by the operator?
ANS: After topping up, the third party service provider may not be able to send the electronic top-up order to the operator in time due to network problems, and hence your operator may not be aware of your top-up. Your operator will top up your data after it receives the order from the third party service provider, and you will be sent a SMS informing you of the successful crediting. Please be sure to begin using your data only after receiving this confirmation SMS so as not to incur any extra data charges.
5. Can I do roaming with the topped up data?
ANS: When you top up your data, look out for the prompts on the page; if it shows “For nationwide use”, it means you can do roaming anywhere within the country.
6. When will the topped up data expire?
ANS: For operators in most provinces, the topped up data is valid within the month and shall expire at the end of the month; some data expires within 30 days, while others are valid for three months; for flexible account users, the data will expire on the monthly billing date. For the exact data expiry date, please refer to the confirmation SMS or check with your operator.
7. Can you cancel a data top-up that has been processed?
ANS: No. Data top-up transactions are almost instant. After making payment, the transaction system will send a top-up request to the operator within seconds, and the top-up will be credited. The orders will be locked during top-ups, and it will not be possible to get a cash refund. Please consider carefully before deciding on the top-up value.
8. Why haven\'t I received the refund after my top-up failed?
ANS: Under normal circumstances, we will refund you immediately if your top-up fails. If you do not receive the refund within 24 hours, please call the customer service hotline: 400-819-9910.
9. Why do I keep failing to top up data for my number?
ANS: China Unicom limits the total number of top-ups per month to 5; after topping up 5 times (not limited to WeChat), subsequent attempts to top-up will be rejected and refunded. In this case, please select data top-ups of higher values. Top-up will also be rejected and refunded for reasons like late payment, conflicting packages, non-real-name authentication, user blacklisted by the operator, etc.
10. How can I check my order or transaction history?
ANS: You can check your orders and transaction history at the top right hand corner of the data top-up page.
11. Why the beginning or ending of months often fail to top-up?
ANS: Make sure not to purchase at the begining or ending of months, in case of your order failed by the busy systems of the mobile operators.
